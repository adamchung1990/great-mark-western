  • HAND-PICKED, HANDLED WITH CARE

    GMW looks to DNA-verified genetics to select specific traits that develop better-than-normal tenderness, marbling and rib-eye size. But good genes only paint part of the picture.

    Ultimately, tenderness is determined by well-orchestrated, hands-on care.

    GMW ranchers go the extra mile, nurturing reputation cattle in ideal environments with specialized care and feeding that naturally enhances each animal’s development potential, all while painstakingly adhering to GMW quality, detail and safety standards.

    It’s this proven cattle-care regimen, augmented with a high-energy corn finishing diet that results in fork-tender, perfectly marbled beef with an enhanced flavor profile.
